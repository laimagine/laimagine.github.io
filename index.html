Lluna

Lluna is a password management system that does NOT store your passwords. In addition to that, Lluna removes all complications involved with creating, remembering, saving and retrieving passwords without having to deal with any of the hassles associated.

  • More Information: http://www.laimagine.com/lluna.html
  • Repository: https://github.com/laimagine/lluna
  • Google Play: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.laimagine.lluna
  • Apple Store: Coming soon

Tablleau

Tablleau is a platform that documents some of the most meaningful tattoos and the stories behind them. Tablleau can be experienced as a blog as well as a mobile app.
